Projects


Hoopers

A mobile-first web app designed to help basketball players find pickup games in their area and track their previous game scores

Hoopers is a mobile web app developed by my team of three for my Interaction & Design class. Using a combination of HTML, CSS, Javascript, Express.js, jQuery, and Bootstrap, our team designed and created a web-app to let users find basketball pickup games in their area and help them track their previous game scores and game statistics.

My primary role in this project was to create HTML pages, style them with CSS, and incorporate Leaflet's API for our map feature. I learned a lot about the power of Twitter's Bootstrap and our team used it extensively throughout our project.

Besides front-end development, our team also focused on UI and UX by performing user testing, storyboards, prototyping, and interviews in order to create a UI and UX friendly app. My team went through several iterations of design before finally settling on our final result.

Special thanks to my teammates: Ashley Chen and Anuraj Dash. I wish them the best in their future endeavors and enjoyed working with them immensely on this project.
